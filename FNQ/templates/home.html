About Us

FitNoQuit is a fitness assistant developed by four curious students of K. J. Somaiya College of Engineering as a part of their Final Year Project. It aims to recommend a personalized and preference-based diet to it's users that is sustainable and satisfies the end goal (weight loss, weight gain, maintain) of the user. The web application also aims to suggest workout to the users based on their comfort and taking into account the time duration they wish to dedicate for the workout. The system also suggests blogs to its users based on their categorical likings and preference.
